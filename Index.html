About

WHO I AM

I am a Creative Producer based in Bogotá, Colombia, with experience in international television, digital content, and live production.

I have worked on large-scale reality TV formats in collaboration with European broadcasters, contributing to production coordination, reward management, and on-site execution within fast-paced, multi-episode environments.

My approach combines structured production management with strong visual awareness. I focus on translating ideas into clear workflows, efficient execution, and high-quality results — across television, digital content, and live experiences.

Alongside my production work, I develop visual and motion-based projects that expand my understanding of digital storytelling and creative direction.

Fluent in Spanish and English. Open to relocation. Available to work with production companies, creative agencies, and multidisciplinary teams.
